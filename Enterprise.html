Revosect (2018-2022)

More than just the world's best aim team.

        Revosect is an esports oriented gaming community with a focus on improvement, training and top tier talent. We create content featuring the best mechanically skilled aimers, educational resources and aim trainer content including benchmarks, tasks and routines for Aim Lab to help you reach your potential in-game.
        We also seek to foster up and coming talent by creating an exclusive group of the best aimers across many games known as Team rA. This is the place to help find players who are willing to train, grow and showcase their talents.
